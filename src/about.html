About me

I'm a digital content manager, programmer, and classical singer based in the greater Chicago area.

My primary interests are in web development. I like using TypeScript, Node, Tailwind, Postgres, Docker, and React. I really like using Sveltekit, CSS, Markdown, SQLite, and Cloudflare. I don't particularly like PHP or writing CSS as JS.

When I'm not programming, I like to sing in vocal ensembles around Chicago and beyond. In 2023, I won a GRAMMY award as a soloist on Born, released by The Crossing, singing the music of Edie Hill.

My wife and I enjoy working together, entertaining, and raising our Mini Australian Shepherd.
