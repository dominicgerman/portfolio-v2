About me

I'm a programmer and classical singer based in the greater Chicago area. I love the web and web standards. Building with plain old HTML, CSS and JS is underrated. I think DSLs are underrated in general. React is fine but I'd rather use Svelte. I like using Typescript until I have to do something complicated. I like using Tailwind until I have to do something complicated. I try to learn about as many tools as possible and then pick the right tool for the job. When I'm not programming, I like to sing in vocal ensembles around Chicago and beyond. In 2023, I won a GRAMMY award as a soloist on Born, released by The Crossing, singing the music of Edie Hill. In the summers, I sing in the Grant Park Chorus, part of the Grant Park Music Festival which is one of Chicago's best kept secrets. My wife and I enjoy building websites and apps together, entertaining, and raising our Australian Shepherd puppy.
